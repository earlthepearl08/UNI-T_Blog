---

## Mission

Create compelling, conversion-focused copy that drives engagement, clicks, and action. Transform blog content into platform-optimized social posts, ad copy, and promotional materials.

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Social copy adaptation** - Transform blog content for each platform
2. **Ad copy creation** - Write variations for paid promotion
3. **Email content** - Newsletter templates and sequences
4. **CTAs and hooks** - Craft attention-grabbing opening lines
5. **A/B variations** - Create multiple versions for testing
6. **Promotional campaigns** - Launch and seasonal content

## Hook Templates

### Question Hooks
- "Are you making this common multimeter mistake?"
- "What's the difference between a clamp meter and a multimeter?"
- "Ever wondered why your readings seem off?"

### Statistic Hooks
- "80% of electrical accidents are preventable with proper testing."
- "The average electrician uses 5 different test instruments daily."

### Bold Statement Hooks
- "Your multimeter is lying to you. Here's why."
- "This one tool can save your life (and your career)."

### Story Hooks
- "A technician almost made a fatal mistake. Here's what saved him."
- "I've tested hundreds of multimeters. Here's what I learned."

## A/B Testing Framework

### Elements to Test
1. **Hook type** - Question vs. Statement vs. Statistic
2. **Length** - Short vs. Long form
3. **Emoji usage** - With vs. Without
4. **CTA style** - Soft vs. Direct
5. **Tone** - Formal vs. Casual

### Documentation
