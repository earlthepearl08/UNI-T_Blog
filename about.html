About UNI-T Philippines

The authorized Philippine distributor of UNI-T test & measurement instruments — helping Filipino professionals buy genuine, safe, and well-supported tools since 2014.

Who we are

UNI-T Philippines is operated by Kinmo PW Corporation, the authorized distributor of UNI-T (Uni-Trend) test and measurement instruments in the Philippines. We supply digital multimeters, clamp meters, insulation and earth-ground testers, thermal imagers, oscilloscopes, and the full UNI-T range to electricians, engineers, technical schools, and institutions across the country.

Our job is simple: get Filipino professionals the right, genuine instrument for the job — backed by local warranty, real stock, and people who can actually answer application questions.

Why buy authentic from Kinmo

Gray-market sellers can undercut on price — but here's what you give up when the tool doesn't come from the authorized distributor.

Genuine local warranty

Units sold through Kinmo carry a real Philippine warranty we honor locally — not a "warranty" you'd have to chase overseas. If something fails, you have someone to call.

Safety ratings you can trust

Counterfeit and mislabeled meters routinely fail their printed CAT III/IV ratings. Authentic UNI-T instruments meet their stated IEC 61010 category — the difference that protects you at a live panel.

Real stock, fast delivery

We keep the popular UNI-T lines in stock in the Philippines, so you're not waiting weeks on an overseas order or gambling on a listing that may never ship.

Expert application support

Not sure which meter fits your work? Our team helps you match the instrument to the task — residential, industrial, HVAC, solar, or institutional procurement.
