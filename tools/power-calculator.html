Power & Load Calculator

Calculate amperes from kW, transpose voltages, and size motors for any 1P or 3P system — with wire, breaker, and instrument recommendations.

Power to Current (kW → Amperes)

Convert load power in kilowatts to running current. Live results update as you type.

Typical: 0.85 motors, 0.95 lighting, 1.0 resistive
Use <1.0 if entering mechanical kW (motor shaft output)
1-Phase: I = (P × 1000) / (V × PF × η)
3-Phase: I = (P × 1000) / (√3 × V × PF × η)

Current to Power (Amperes → kW)

Reverse calculator — useful when measuring with a clamp meter and you need real power.

Use a power-quality meter for accurate PF measurement
1-Phase: P = V × I × PF / 1000 (kW)
3-Phase: P = √3 × V × I × PF / 1000 (kW)

Motor HP Calculator

Quick reference for motor full-load amps (FLA), locked-rotor amps (LRA), and starter sizing.

Continuous-duty motors: 1.15. Standard: 1.0
FLA (1P): I = (HP × 746) / (V × PF × η)
FLA (3P): I = (HP × 746) / (√3 × V × PF × η)
LRA ≈ 6 × FLA (Code G/H letter typical)
